Whatsong

Official repo of the WhatSong Android app

View project on GitHub

Tired of choosing between Google Search and the other music providers? You can have them all!

With WhatSong, you will have all the different providers at your fingertips. With unlimited shortcuts, DashClock implementation and a special Google Now-alike gesture, you can have unlimited possibilities!

Designed by Lydia Selimalhigazi

Supported music providers

  • Sound Search
  • Shazam
  • Shazam Encore
  • SoundHound
  • SoundHound Pro
  • TrackID
  • musiXmatch
  • SoundTracking
